ORBIT Privacy Policy Effective date: 14 June 2026

ORBIT is developed and published by Enaam Malik.

1. Data collection

ORBIT does not collect, transmit, sell, rent or share personal data. The game does not contain advertising, analytics, tracking technology, user accounts or third-party software development kits that collect user information.

2. Local game data

High scores, settings, statistics and cosmetic unlocks are stored locally on the user's device. This information is not sent to the developer or any third party. Users can remove this local data by clearing ORBIT's app storage or uninstalling the game.

3. Internet access

ORBIT does not request Android internet permission and is designed to work offline.

4. Purchases

ORBIT is sold as a paid download through Google Play. Google processes the purchase. The developer does not receive or store the user's payment-card details. Google's handling of purchase information is governed by Google's own privacy policies.

5. Children's privacy

ORBIT does not knowingly collect personal data from children or adults because it does not collect personal data from any user.

6. Data security, retention and deletion

No personal data is retained by the developer. Local game progress remains on the device until the user clears the app's storage or uninstalls the app.

7. Changes to this policy

If ORBIT's data practices change, this policy and the Google Play Data safety declaration will be updated before the changed version is released.

8. Contact

Privacy enquiries: studioorbit.support@gmail.com

This policy applies to the Android game ORBIT, package name com.enaam.orbit.
